Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-18

This policy explains what data Tova通话 collects, why, and how you can control it. Tova通话 is published by the developer operating under the contact email listed below; "we", "us", and "our" refer to that same entity.

1. Data we collect

  • Text you ask us to translate, plus the source and target language codes. This is sent over TLS to our backend, which forwards it to the translation engine you have selected (Google Translate by default, optionally DeepL, OpenAI, or Baidu on Pro). Translation text is not persisted on our servers beyond the request lifetime, and is not used to train models.
  • OCR results. Images captured by the camera are processed on-device using Apple VisionKit and Google ML Kit. Only the recognised text string is transmitted to the backend; the image itself never leaves your device.
  • Voice input. When you use the Speak tab, transcription happens on-device via Apple Speech. The recognised text is sent to the translation backend the same way typed text is.
  • App settings, favourites, glossary, and history. Stored locally on your device via SharedPreferences and SQLite. Never transmitted, except that glossary entries can be synced between your own iCloud-linked devices via Apple's NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore (iCloud Key-Value Store).
  • Apple In-App Purchase receipts. Receipts are sent to our backend only for server-side verification, per Apple guidelines. No card data is ever seen by us.
  • Crash reports. If the app crashes, a signature (error message + top 8 stack frames) and light environment data (OS version, UI language, active service, Pro status, last screen) are sent to our backend for diagnostics. No personal identifiers and no message content.
  • Feedback you submit in-app. The message body, the email address you provide, and light environment data (OS, active language, Pro status). Used to reply to you and triage issues.
  • Advertising identifiers (free tier only). If you grant App Tracking Transparency permission, the iOS advertising identifier (IDFA) is shared with Google AdMob to deliver personalised ads. If you deny permission, only non-personalised ads are served.

2. Data we do NOT collect

  • Your name or contact details, unless you voluntarily submit them via in-app feedback or email us directly.
  • Precise location. We do not request location permission.
  • Camera roll contents beyond the specific image you choose to translate.
  • Health, financial, or biometric data.

3. Third parties

  • Translation engines. Text you translate is routed through the engine you select: Google Translate, DeepL, OpenAI, or Baidu. Their respective privacy policies apply to text routed through them.
  • Apple. For In-App Purchase, Text-to-Speech, Speech-to-Text, VisionKit, the Translation framework, iCloud Key-Value Store, and the App Store.
  • Google AdMob (free tier only). For serving advertising. AdMob may collect the data described in Apple's ATT prompt if you grant permission.
  • Gmail SMTP for transactional email (your feedback receipts and internal support notifications).
  • We do not share data with data brokers.

4. Retention

Translation requests are not retained on our servers. Feedback entries you submit and crash reports are retained for up to 24 months for triage and product-quality purposes, then deleted. Favourites, glossary terms, and scan history live exclusively on your device; uninstalling the app removes them.
